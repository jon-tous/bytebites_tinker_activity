# ByteBites Tinker Activity

## Reflection for helping students

This tinker is a helpful exercise for students in modeling a domain with an AI-first workflow, designing and refining in conversation with AI before implementing and testing as well. Students may struggle in finding the right balance of steering the direction of the design and implementation, and giving the AI the appropriate level of context and constraints. I found the custom AI agent to be helpful in general, although it originally output a different UML format than the default agent, so I realized the UML format would be a good additional instruction in the agent definition. Students could be guided for example to consider how they would design the system before being influenced by AI's approach.
